LIQUIDITY RATIOS

Liquidity ratio is formulated to measure a company’s ability to meet its short-term financial obligations. This ratio is calculated to test whether a company’s resources able to pay its creditor when payments are due. Two commonly use liquidity ratios are current ratio and acid-test ratio.
